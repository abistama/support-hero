About Abistama

We're all about making your work life a whole lot easier and a bit more fun. We're a small but mighty team of tech enthusiasts, passionate about creating seamless integrations and innovative solutions for your favorite tools like Slack and Jira.

Simple Solutions

We create tools that solve real problems. Abistama turns Slack reactions into Jira tickets, making project management effortless.

User-Driven

Our users' needs guide our development. We built Abistama because teams needed an easier way to track tasks.

Tech for Everyone

We believe powerful tools should be easy to use. Abistama brings advanced project management to all team members, regardless of technical skill.

Sustainable Growth

As a bootstrapped company, we focus on creating value for our users rather than chasing rapid expansion.

Know About Abistama

Our journey began with a simple idea: what if creating a Jira ticket was as easy as reacting to a Slack message? This spark of innovation has grown into Abistama, a company dedicated to streamlining workflows and enhancing productivity across organizations.

We believe that the future of work lies in intelligent automation and seamless integration. Our mission is to empower teams to focus on what truly matters by eliminating tedious manual processes. With Abistama, we're not just saving time; we're fostering a culture of responsiveness and continuous improvement.

From startups to enterprises, we're committed to tailoring our solution to meet the unique needs of each organization. Our journey is one of constant evolution, driven by user feedback and technological advancements. Join us in shaping the future of work, one emoji at a time.
